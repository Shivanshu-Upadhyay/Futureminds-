Courses Description :

"Aiming to improve the technology of the organization? Become an ethical hacker. Ethical hacking works for the organization by helping you to identify security vulnerabilities. From big MNCs to other new budding organizations, everyone needs an ethical hacker "

What Will I Learn? :

  Introduction to Ethical Hacking.

  Footprints and Reconnaissance !

  Scanning Networks .

  Enumeration .

  Vulnerability Analysis .

  System Hacking.

  Malware Threats !

  Sniffing .

  Social Engineering .

  Denial-of-services .

  Session Hijacking .

  Evading IDS, Firewalls, and Honeypots !

  Hacking Web Servers .

  Hacking Web Applications .

  SQL Injection .

  Hacking Wireless Networks.

Learning Outcomes :

This course will make you a certified Ethical Hacker that has mastered advanced network packet analysis and system penetration testing techniques. By the end you will have a complete skill set of building network security. After completing his course you would have enough hands-on experience and would have mastered the techniques that hackers use to penetrate network systems and fortify your system against it. Not just this, you will be rewarded with a completion certificate and guaranteed internship opportunities on the go.
